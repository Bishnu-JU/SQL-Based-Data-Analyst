# SQL-Based-Data-Analyst

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the online sales data of **Monday Coffee**, a coffee company that has been selling products across major Indian cities since **January 2023**.

The objective was to identify the **top three Indian cities for opening new coffee shop locations** by analyzing customer demand, sales performance, population, rental costs, and market potential using SQL.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Estimate coffee consumers in each city based on population.
- Analyze revenue generated during the last quarter of 2023.
- Identify the best-selling coffee products.
- Measure customer spending across different cities.
- Evaluate market potential for new store expansion.
- Recommend the top cities for new coffee shop locations.

---

## 📊 Business Questions Solved

- Estimated coffee consumers in each city (assuming **25%** of the population consumes coffee).
- Total revenue generated during **Q4 2023**.
- Total units sold for each coffee product.
- Average sales amount per customer in each city.
- City-wise population and estimated coffee consumers.
- Top 3 selling products in every city.
- Number of unique customers in each city.
- Average sales per customer vs average rent per customer.
- Month-over-month sales growth analysis.
- Market potential analysis based on sales, customers, rent, and coffee consumers.

## 📈 Key Insights

- **Pune** generated the highest revenue while maintaining one of the lowest rent-per-customer ratios.
- **Delhi** has the largest estimated coffee-consuming population (approximately **7.7 million**) and a strong customer base.
- **Jaipur** recorded one of the highest customer counts with low rental costs and strong average customer spending.
- Premium coffee products consistently contributed the largest share of sales across major cities.

---

# 📍 Recommended Cities for Expansion

| Rank | City | Reason |
|------|------|--------|
| 🥇 1 | Pune | Highest total revenue, high average sales per customer, and low rent per customer |
| 🥈 2 | Delhi | Largest estimated coffee consumer base (~7.7M), strong customer count, and affordable rent |
| 🥉 3 | Jaipur | High customer count, low rental cost, and excellent average sales per customer |
